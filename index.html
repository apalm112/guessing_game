The Awesome Guessing Game


Here's a short JavaScript powered guessing game, which will ask the user for answers to six questions. It will give a bit of feedback to their answers as well.


Am I new to coding?

Have I read the Lord of the Rings trilogy?

Is my favorite sport cycling?

How old am I?

Have I ridden from Seattle to Portland in one day?

How many Tour de France's did Greg LeMond win?
